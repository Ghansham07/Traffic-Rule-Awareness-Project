Traffic Signs

Stop Sign

This sign indicates that you must come to a complete stop and ensure the way is clear before proceeding.

Yield Sign

This sign means you must slow down and yield to other traffic. Stop if necessary.

Speed Limit Sign

This sign indicates the maximum (50km/hr) speed you are legally allowed to travel on this section of road.

No Entry

This sign indicates that entry is not allowed for all types of vehicles.

Priority for Oncoming Traffic

This sign indicates that oncoming traffic has priority over your traffic.

All Motor Vehicles Prohibited

This sign indicates that all types of motor vehicles are prohibited.

Truck Prohibited

This sign indicates that trucks are prohibited.

Height Limit

Length Limit
